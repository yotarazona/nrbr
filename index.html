Welcome

The Normalized Radar Burn Ratio (NRBR) improves burned area mapping in cloud-prone regions using Sentinel-1 radar data.

About NRBR

NRBR is a new radar index calculated as:
NRBR = (RBRVH - RBRVV) / (RBRVH + RBRVV)

It enhances the contrast between burned and unburned areas, especially where optical imagery is limited.

Methodology

  • Sentinel-1 pre- and post-fire image acquisition
  • Speckle and topographic correction
  • Calculation of RBRVV and RBRVH
  • NRBR index computation
  • Burned area segmentation using U-Net deep learning

Results

NRBR was evaluated across Portugal, Spain, and California. In Portugal, NRBR achieved a Dice Coefficient of 84.4% versus 72.3% for dNBR.
